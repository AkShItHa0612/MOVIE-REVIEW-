Action/Drama :

Rangasthalam

Aravinda Sametha Veera Raghava

A young man sets out to avenge his father's death but soon realises that violence is not the key to his salvation. His life changes when he meets a young woman, who helps him rediscover himself.

Naa Peru Surya, Naa Illu India

A soldier aspiring to be posted at the LOC suffers from anger management issues, due to which he gets court-martialled. He then sets out to meet a psychologist whose approval can get him back on duty.

Comedy :

Chalo

Hari and Karthika fall in love with each other but are distraught when they find they are from different castes. However, they decide to fight for their relationship and parents' approval.

Silly Fellows

Veerababu and his best friend try to impress an MLA for their own advantage. However, they land in trouble on learning that the MLA knows the whereabouts of an illegal stash of money.

Achari America Yatra

After being accused of murder, a young priest manipulates his associates to flee to America in order to chase the love of his life.

Romance :

Hello Guru Prema Kosame

Hushaaru

Horror :
